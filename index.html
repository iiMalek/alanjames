ABOUT ME

Welcome to Alan James Piano Lessons, where music meets mastery.


With over a decade of experience as a piano teacher, I'm passionate about helping students of all ages and skill levels discover the beauty of playing the piano.


My personalized approach to teaching emphasizes technique, theory, and musicianship, ensuring that every student has a strong foundation for their musical journey.


Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an advanced player seeking to refine your skills, I'm dedicated to helping you achieve your musical goals.


At Alan James Piano Lessons, we believe that learning music should be fun, engaging, and rewarding. Let's make beautiful music together.
